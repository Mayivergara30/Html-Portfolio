About Me

With experience in design areas, María Carolina Vergara Vanegas is a graphic designer and marketing specialist with over a decade of experience in branding, editorial design, infographics, and academic project coordination. She holds a Master’s degree in Marketing and a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design from Universidad Autónoma del Caribe.

Currently serving as the Internationalization Coordinator at Universidad Simón Bolívar, she leads global academic collaborations, manages institutional communications, and integrates creative strategies into international education projects. Her background also includes hands-on experience in publishing and print production, overseeing the design and layout of brochures, flyers, POP materials, and corporate manuals.

In 2024, she worked as a freelance contractor for the multinational branding agency Mother Tongue, contributing to corporate identity and visual branding projects. She is proficient in the Adobe Creative Suite, especially InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop, and skilled in preparing professional print and digital materials. María Carolina is known for combining creative vision with strategic thinking, and for her ability to work seamlessly across academic, corporate, and multicultural environments.
